Hazelnut Nest

Description

Ingredients

  • 100 ml water
  • 100g sugar
  • 75g butter
  • 50g dark chocolate
  • 50g hazelnut croknate
  • 140g ground plasma
  • hazelnut cream
  • hazelnut crunch

Steps

  1. Water and sugar put in a pot to boil.
  2. When sugar disolves, add butter and chocolate.
  3. Add hazelnut croknate and ground plasma.
  4. When mixture cools down a little bit, we make balls in which we insert a small hole.
  5. Roll the balls in a hazelnut crunch and fill with hazelnut cream.
  6. Eat them all!
