Hot chocolate

Description

Ingredients (for one cup/glass)

  • 50g of quality chocolate
  • 55ml sweet cream
  • 170ml milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla pudding
  • few drops of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Chocolate and sweet cream dissolve on low heat.
  2. Vanilla pudinng dissolve in little water, and then pour in milk.
  3. Mix everything constantly on medium heat until mixture is thick enough.
  4. Add few drops of vanilla extract
  5. If desired, decorate with wipped cream, ground plasma, marshmallows, and so on.
  6. Enjoy!
