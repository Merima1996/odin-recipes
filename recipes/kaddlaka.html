Kaddlaka

Description

Ingredients

  • 70g butter
  • 100g chocolate
  • 20g hazelnuts
  • 2 eggs
  • 70g white sugar
  • 70g brown sugar
  • 2 spoons of flour

Steps

  1. Melt butter and chocolate at low temperature.
  2. Mix eggs and sugar in a bowl, and add melted butter and chocolate. Add hezelnuts to the mixture.
  3. Combine everything and add flour.
  4. Put baking paper in an 18cm mold and pour the mixture.
  5. Bake at 180 degrees celsius for about 20 min.
  6. Decorate with strawbeeries as desired.
  7. Have a good time eating!
